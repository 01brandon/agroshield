about agroshield

feeding the future with intelligent farming

ai-powered food security for smallholder farmers across africa and beyond.

our mission

bridging the gap between farmers and technology

over 700 million people face chronic hunger globally, yet smallholder farmers — who produce roughly 70% of the world's food — operate almost entirely without access to the tools that could help them.

agroshield was built to close that gap. we combine ai, satellite imagery, real-time weather data, and mobile payments into a single platform that any farmer can use — whether they have a smartphone or a basic feature phone.

join agroshield today

what we do

20 modules. one platform. every farmer.

from ai-powered crop disease detection and hyperlocal weather alerts to a live produce marketplace with m-pesa escrow and parametric insurance that pays out automatically — agroshield covers every part of a farmer's working life.

farmers without smartphones can access everything via ussd on a basic phone. farmers with smartphones get a full progressive web app. ngo and government partners get a geospatial analytics dashboard.
